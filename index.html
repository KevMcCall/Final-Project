About The Owner


Hi! My name is Mara McCall and I am the owner of iYarnyou. I started my small business this year, 2020 of March. One of my hobbies is to crochet. My mother is the one that taught me the skill when I was still in elementary school. I am really grateful that I was raised in a Filipino way. Strict, disciplined, cultured, and yet you can still have fun.

I was born and raised in the Philippines and I've been here in the U.S. for 2 years now. My husband always inspires me to do the things that I love and to achieve my goals.

This hobby turned into a business because of one of my friends asked me to make a table cloth as a gift for her mother-in-law. Then, my best friend asked me for one as well, and so on, and so forth...

I really enjoy crocheting. It's rewarding to see how the customers react to my work. That's why one of my goals is to make this business grow larger and one day help housewives, single mothers, or just anyone who wants to learn how to crochet. I continue everyday to learn and grow in what I'm passionate about.
